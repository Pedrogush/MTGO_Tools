Sideboard Guide

The Sideboard Guide tab inside the Deck Workspace lets you record matchup-by-matchup sideboarding plans for the loaded deck. Entries are saved automatically and reloaded whenever you open the same deck.

Adding an entry

  1. Click Add Entry.
  2. Type the opponent archetype name.
  3. List the cards to bring IN (from the sideboard) and the cards to take OUT (from the mainboard).
  4. Click OK to save.

Editing and removing entries

Select an entry in the guide list, then click Edit Entry to modify it or Remove Entry to delete it.

Exclusions

Click Edit Exclusions to specify cards that should never appear in the sideboard guide for this deck (e.g. basic lands or cards you do not side with any matchup).

Flex slots

Click Edit Flex Slots to mark cards whose count varies between matchups. Flex slot cards are displayed with a special indicator in the guide, reminding you to adjust their numbers on a matchup-by-matchup basis.

Pinning the guide

Click Pin Guide to open a compact, floating version of the guide that remains visible while you browse other tabs or windows. This is useful for reviewing plans mid-match.

Export and import

ActionDescription
Export CSV Saves the entire guide to a CSV file that can be opened in a spreadsheet application or shared with teammates.
Import CSV Loads a guide from a previously exported CSV file, replacing the current guide.
