Metagame Analysis

The Metagame Analysis window gives a top-level breakdown of the competitive format currently selected in the main window. Open it by clicking Metagame Analysis in the toolbar.

Contents

  • Archetype share chart — a visual breakdown of how much of the metagame each archetype represents, based on MTGGoldfish data.
  • Win-rate table — lists archetypes with their reported win rates.
  • Trend indicators — where data is available, shows whether an archetype is trending up or down.

Refreshing data

Data is fetched from MTGGoldfish and cached locally. Click Reload inside the window or use Reload Archetypes in the main research panel to pull fresh data.
