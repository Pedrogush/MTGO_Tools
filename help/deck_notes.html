Deck Notes

The Deck Notes tab inside the Deck Workspace provides a free-form text area where you can jot down anything relevant to the loaded deck: card choices, tournament results, matchup thoughts, or links to articles.

Saving notes

Notes are saved automatically when you navigate away from the tab or close the application. They are stored locally and reloaded whenever you open the same deck again.

Notes per deck

Each deck has its own independent notes. Switching to a different deck clears the notes area and loads that deck’s saved notes. Notes are linked to the deck by its name and format.
