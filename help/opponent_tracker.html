Opponent Tracker

The Opponent Tracker is a floating window opened from the toolbar. It detects your current opponent’s username from the MTGO window title and looks up which archetypes they most commonly play.

Opening the tracker

Click Opponent Tracker in the toolbar. The window can remain open throughout your MTGO session.

How detection works

The tracker reads the active MTGO window title, which MTGO updates to include the opponent’s username during a match. No MTGO SDK or API key is required — only the window title is read.

Results

Once an opponent is detected, the tracker shows:

  • The opponent’s username.
  • A list of archetypes associated with that player, based on scraped tournament results.
  • A compact sideboard guide preview if you have a guide entry for any of their likely archetypes.
  • A radar chart summarising the most common cards across their known archetypes.

Limitations

  • Detection only works while MTGO is running and a game is active.
  • Results depend on public tournament data; players who rarely enter events may show no data.
